The Founders

Built with Purpose.

Cement Creek Vet is a small-animal practice built from scratch by Dr. Kara Erickson and Shawna Castillo, business partners and besties with over 35 combined years of experience in veterinary medicine. We each spent the last few decades working in the industry and taking notes — on what worked, what didn't, and what a really good vet visit could actually feel like for both you and your pet.

Our mission is to leave every patient, client, and interaction better than we found it — through honest medicine, empathy, and real conversations.

Everything is intentional.

Decades of notes on what worked, what didn't, and what a really good vet visit could actually feel like — built into how we practice every day.

01 · Approach

Context, not just conclusions.

When we recommend something, we tell you why — how it works, what the evidence says, what happens if you skip it. You'll never leave with an estimate you don't understand or a diagnosis without knowing what it means for your animal. Our collaborative approach to treatment plans means you won't feel bad for asking questions or cornered by the cost. We work with CareCredit, Klarna, Cherry payment plans, and even created our application-based Heart Dog & Cat Fund, so that you always have options.

02 · Care

Personalized pet care.

Every patient is treated as an individual — their own history, their own needs, their own plan.

03 · Handling

Gentle handling as a standard, not a specialty.

We prioritize a low-stress experience for all, and adapt our handling techniques based on your pet's disposition, lifestyle, and personality.

The fishbowl, mid-build.

Modern tools and technology support.

In-house labwork, advanced diagnostic imaging, dedicated dental and surgical suites, and our personal favorite — a glass observation room affectionately called the “fishbowl,” so your pet is never out of our sight during their time here.
