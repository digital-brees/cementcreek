Dental disease is the thing we wish more pet owners knew about earlier. Most pets have some degree of it by age three, and most of them won't show it. That's why we built a dedicated dental suite with digital dental imaging – so we can see exactly what's happening below the gumline, not just guess at it.

Therapeutic Laser

Therapeutic laser uses targeted light energy to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and accelerate healing. We use it for post-surgical recovery, wound care, and a range of musculoskeletal conditions. It's non-invasive, well-tolerated by most pets, and often makes a noticeable difference – especially for older dogs and cats managing chronic pain.
